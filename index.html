Important COVID-19 Update

Please note that currently the Chief Dental Officer has advised:

  • Cancellation of appointments for vulnerable groups (other patients can also cancel if they wish to do so).
  • Services cannot be provided to those with potential COVID symptoms or those in their households.
  • Avoid all Aerosol Generating Procedures wherever possible. (Drilling/scaling)

With this in mind, we currently remain open, following this advice, with limited scope to provide basic examinations/treatment/advice.

Should you have any concerns regarding how this affects your care, please do not hesitate to contact the practice to discuss on 01642 710 421.

The situation is rapidly changing, we endeavour to keep you up to date as the pandemic develops.
